Service

Dental Implant

A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed in the jawbone to support a crown, replacing a missing tooth permanently and functionally.

Dental Filling

A dental filling is a restorative material used to repair cavities or damaged teeth, restoring their normal function, shape, and preventing further decay.

Dental Crown

A dental crown is a custom-made cap placed over a damaged tooth to restore its shape, size, strength, function, and natural appearance.

Dental Bridges

A dental bridge is a fixed dental restoration used to replace 1 or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent natural teeth or implants.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic procedure that removes stains, brightens teeth, improves smile appearance, and boosts confidence.

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction removes a damaged or painful tooth to prevent infection, relieve discomfort, and maintain overall oral health.
